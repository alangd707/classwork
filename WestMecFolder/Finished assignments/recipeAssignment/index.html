Easy Protein shake recipe!

This recipe is used to create an easy, cheap, and protein dense banana shake that tastes very good!

Protein: 70g, Carbs: 40g, Fat: 40g.

    Ingredients:

  • 3 scoops Gold Standard 100% Whey (Vanilla Icecream)
  • 10 floz. Milk
  • 1 Banana(any size, barely affects flavor), or 30 Frozen Blueberries
  • 4 Icecubes

    Instructions:

  1. Measure 10floz. of milk, put it inside blender
  2. Measure 3 scoops of protein powder, put it inside blender
  3. Peel and put 1 banana into the blender. If you wish to try another flavor, instead of the banana, use 30 frozen blueberries
  4. Put 4 icecubes into the blender
  5. Blend until no visible chunks
  6. Enjoy!

Why use this?

What's so effective about this protein shake is the ratio of carbs and fats to protein, as this shake takes 3 scoops, this is very high in protein and is low in fats and carbs. If you wish to dilute the density of protein, add another banana or add more blueberries. If you wish to store this, put it in a fridge so it stays cold and the ice doesn't melt. Enjoy :)
